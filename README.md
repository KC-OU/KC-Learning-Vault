This repository is more than just a collection of notes; it is a structured roadmap for mastering IT disciplines, from foundational hardware support to complex network automation and offensive security.

---

## 🎨 A Visual Mastery: The WOTC-Beyond Aesthetic

The KC - Learning Vault is built using a heavily customized **Obsidian** environment, utilizing the **ITS Theme** with a **WOTC-Beyond (Dungeons & Dragons 5e)** skin. 

- **Authentic RPG Look:** Notes are styled like official D&D sourcebooks, featuring parchment backgrounds, classic serif typography, and decorative "burnt paper" borders.
- **Dynamic Callouts:** Custom info-boxes (WOTC-style) provide key certification data, costs, and exam codes.
- **Interactive Progress:** Tracking exams and lab completion with a tabletop-RPG feel.

## 📁 The Three Pillars of the Vault

### 1. 🏛️ The Education Vault
Houses a massive library of vendor-neutral and vendor-specific training modules.

*   **CompTIA Pathway:** Foundation to Advanced (A+, Network+, Security+, Linux+, CySA+, PenTest+).
*   **Cisco Track:** Technical depth in infrastructure (CCST, CCNA, CCNA Cyber).
*   **Cloud & AI:** Modern modules for AWS, Azure, and Generative AI integration.

### 2. 🛠️ The Practical Vault
Interactive lab environments focusing on real-world application.

*   **TryHackMe:** Guided learning paths (Pre-Security, Offensive Pentesting).
*   **HackTheBox:** Independent research on standalone virtual machines (Machines, ProLabs).
*   **CTF Walkthroughs:** Detailed documentation of exploits, scans, and privilege escalation.

### 3. 📜 The Credential Vault
A roadmap to industry-recognized certifications, detailing cost, exam structure, and professional career alignment.
